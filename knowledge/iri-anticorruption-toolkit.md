# Anti-Corruption Toolkit for Civic Activists (IRI)
Source: https://www.iri.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/iri-anticorruption_toolkit_1204.pdf
Category: Anti-corruption advocacy toolkit
Organization: International Republican Institute (IRI)

## Overview
A toolkit for civic activists that covers research, advocacy, collaboration, and media engagement to push for reform.

## Key modules
- Research methods and security (open source, records, interviews).
- Advocacy and collaboration with coalition building.
- Communications and media strategies.
- Sample meeting agenda and action plans in the appendix.

## Example uses
- Plan evidence gathering with legal and digital safety checks.
- Build a coalition with shared goals and decision rules.
- Draft a communications plan and policy paper outline.
