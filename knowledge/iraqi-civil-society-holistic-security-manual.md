# Holistic Security Manual for Women HRDs in Iraq
Source: https://www.iraqicivilsociety.org/archives/14378
Category: Security manual and training
Organization: Iraqi Civil Society Solidarity Initiative

## Overview
A manual for women human rights defenders in Iraq that addresses risks across physical, digital, and psychosocial dimensions.

## Key components
- Threat analysis and risk assessment tools.
- Guidance on digital, physical, and psychosocial security.
- Scenario-based responses for protests, online abuse, and smear campaigns.
- References to local Iraqi support services.

## Example uses
- Facilitate a collective risk assessment with a local group.
- Prepare response plans for online harassment or doxxing.
- Integrate psychosocial support into long-term activism planning.
