# World Liberty Congress Handbooks
Source: https://worldlibertycongress.org/handbook/
Category: Political prisoner support
Organization: World Liberty Congress

## Overview
Two handbooks for political prisoners and their communities: "Pathway to Freedom" and "After Liberation." They include advice, strategies, and testimonies and are available in English, Spanish, and French.

## Example uses
- Support families with practical steps toward release.
- Build advocacy and legal support plans for detainees.
- Plan reintegration support for former prisoners.

## Notes
- The page emphasizes guidance from prisoners, lawyers, and activists.
