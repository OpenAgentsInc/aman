# Paskoocheh App Store
Source: https://paskoocheh.com/
Category: App store for APK files and digital security tools
Organization: Paskoocheh

## Overview
An app marketplace focused on distributing free apps that are hard to access due to sanctions or filtering. The catalog includes circumvention tools and popular messaging apps.

## Catalog signals
- Categories include circumvention tools, social and messaging apps, and utilities.
- Popular listings include Psiphon, Lantern, Tor Browser, Outline, and Telegram.

## Example uses
- Provide a trusted place to download APKs when official stores are blocked.
- Offer alternative access to security tools in censored environments.
- Curate recommended tools for a training or onboarding guide.

## Notes
- The interface is in Persian.
- Treat downloads as sensitive in high-risk contexts and verify sources.
