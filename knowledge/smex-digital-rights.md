# SMEX Digital Rights (WANA)
Source: http://smex.org/
Category: Digital rights organization
Organization: SMEX

## Overview
SMEX is a nonprofit that advances digital rights in West Asia and North Africa (WANA). It focuses on freedom of expression, privacy, and user safety online.

## Key programs
- Digital Safety Helpdesk for users facing digital threats.
- Digital Forensics Lab for investigation support.
- Digital Rights Fund and fellowship programs.

## Example uses
- Refer at-risk users to the safety helpdesk.
- Use research and reporting for advocacy on censorship or surveillance.
- Apply for small grants to support local digital rights work.
