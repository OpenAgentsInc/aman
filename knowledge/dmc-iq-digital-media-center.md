# Digital Media Center (DMC) Iraq
Source: https://dmc-iq.com/
Category: Digital media analysis and reporting
Organization: Digital Media Center (Iraq)

## Overview
An Arabic-language site that publishes reports, articles, and alerts on digital platforms, digital government, digital security, AI, and related topics in Iraq.

## Example uses
- Track social media usage trends in Iraq.
- Monitor alerts about platform policy changes or digital risks.
- Use local statistics and case studies in regional reports.

## Notes
- The site navigation includes sections for digital platforms, security, AI, and studies.
