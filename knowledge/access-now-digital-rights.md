# Access Now
Source: https://www.accessnow.org/
Category: Digital rights organization
Organization: Access Now

## Overview
Access Now defends digital rights globally through technical support, advocacy, grassroots grantmaking, and convenings such as RightsCon.

## Key initiatives
- #KeepItOn coalition against internet shutdowns.
- Focus areas include freedom of expression, digital censorship, and resistance to digital authoritarianism.

## Example uses
- Use #KeepItOn resources to document or respond to shutdowns.
- Track reports and updates for regional advocacy.
- Engage with RightsCon for coalition building.

## Notes
- The site highlights ongoing campaigns and regional focus areas.
