# Iran's Authoritarian Playbook (GMF/ASD)
Source: https://securingdemocracy.gmfus.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/Irans_Authoritarian_Playbook.pdf
Category: Influence operations research
Organization: Alliance for Securing Democracy, German Marshall Fund

## Overview
A report on Iran's influence operations, tactics, doctrine, and objectives. It examines how Iran uses information operations, cyber activity, and other tools to undermine democratic institutions.

## Key themes
- Objectives and doctrine behind influence operations.
- Information operations across traditional and social media.
- Asymmetric tools and partnerships with other authoritarian actors.
- Methodology based on government sources and open-source research.

## Example uses
- Brief staff on common influence-operation patterns.
- Create a risk assessment for media manipulation in a campaign.
- Use the methodology section to guide a research plan.
