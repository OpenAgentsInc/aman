# Human Rights and Business Toolkit for Syria (SLDP)
Source: https://sldp.ngo/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/Toolkit-EN.pdf
Category: Human rights documentation toolkit
Organization: Syrian Legal Development Programme (SLDP)

## Overview
A toolkit to help human rights defenders working on Syria identify, document, and advocate on human rights issues linked to business activity.

## Key sections
- Documentation standards and evidence handling.
- Identifying business-related human rights abuses.
- Assessing state involvement and legal context.
- Documenting compliance with business responsibility to prevent abuses.

## Example uses
- Document supply chain or procurement links to rights abuses.
- Build case files with consistent evidence-handling practices.
- Assess how state policy enables corporate abuse.
